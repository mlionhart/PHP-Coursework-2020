About Bruce's Drum Shop

"Bruce has been drumming for over 40 years!"

Bruce has been drumming since the 4th grade! His first snare drum was a Ludwig Acrolite which his father purchased for him. Before Bruce had a real set of drums he used cardboard chlorine canisters!

Bruce's first drum set was part Ludwig and part Rogers. As a reward for getting good grades in school Bruce's parents would buy him another piece for his drum kit. Let's just say that Bruce started getting grades in school as a result of this.

In 1998 Bruce decided to share his passion for vintage and collectible drums by opening up Bruce's Drum Shop in Vancouver, WA.
