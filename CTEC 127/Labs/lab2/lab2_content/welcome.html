Old Skool all the Way!

At Bruce's we only buy and sell vintage drums from the 1960's and 1970's. Whether you are looking at a set of Rogers, Slingerland, Gretsch, Ludwig or other vintage brand, Bruce's is where you will most likely find what you are looking for!

Whether you are looking for an entire kit or just a piece or two - we most likely will have it. If we do not have it, we can absolutely find it for you!
